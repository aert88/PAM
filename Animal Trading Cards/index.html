Honey Badger(aka Crazy NastyAss Honey Badger)

Honey badgers are jet black except for the gray mantle, separated by a white stripe, extending from the crown to the base of the tail.

  • Scientific Name:Mellivora capensis
  • Average Length:23 to 28 cm
  • Average Lifespan:5 to 8 years in wild, 24 years in captivity
  • Habitat: Wide tolerance, from semi-desert to rainforest

Has a fairly long body, but is distinctly thick-set and broad across the back. Its skin is remarkably loose, and allows it to turn and twist freely within it. The honey badger has short and sturdy legs, with five toes on each foot. The feet are armed with very strong claws, which are short on the hind legs and remarkably long on the forelimbs.
